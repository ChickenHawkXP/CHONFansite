CHON is a progressive rock/instrumental rock/math rock band from Oceanside California. The members consist of; Mario Camarena (guitarist), Erick Hansel (guitarist), Nathan Camarena (drums) and Esiah Camarena (bass). They currently have two albums and two E.Ps.
